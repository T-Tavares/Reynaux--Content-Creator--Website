About

césar reynaux

creative director

Hello, My name is César Reynaux, I am a creative director focusing on audiovisual work based in São Paulo. I have 5 years of experience as a designer, and currently, I have been working in the audiovisual field for 6 years. I have had the opportunity to collaborate on projects for brands such as WhatsApp, Rainha, NBA, Reserva, HypeBeast, and Insider, as well as with artists like Far From Alaska, Day Limns, and Gustavo Bertoni. My journey as a designer has provided me with a solid understanding of aesthetics, essential to achieve the desired final results in each project. In addition to working on project development, I also engage in activities such as capturing, editing, and colorization. My goal is to solve brand problems by creating films that positively impact the audience's lives, whether through a lifestyle change or by promoting products targeted to a specific audience. I firmly believe that good ideas generate excellent results.
